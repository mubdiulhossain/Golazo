Golazo is for students who want to be extraordinary

Golazo's dashboard guarantee you to keep tracks of your projects and assignment in exciting, versatile and pleasing way

Key Features

Set SMART goals

SMART refers to Specific, Measurable, Attainable, Realistic and Timely.
Golazo is here to help you to set your SMART goals in an organized manner.
With just a click, you can add, edit and delete the works assigned to you.

Track progress of tasks

After adding the tasks, you can track the progress of each task. The progress
bar will show the percentage of the task completion. The best part is that
the progress tracking can also be done offline!

Track time spent on tasks

You can track the time spent on each task. This means that you will know which
task is more time-consuming. So, you can manage your time properly.

Create reminders

Now, you do not have to worry about having to remember all of the deadlines,
because Golazo allows you to create reminders to encourage timely completion
of tasks. This makes your life a lot easier for sure!

Invite others to monitor progress

Another great feature is that you can invite others to monitor your progress
Your lecturera, friends and even your parents can view your progress, only
if you grant them permission. They can also give comments on your dashboard.
